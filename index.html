Zheng Wang, PhD


（中文名：王政）

A data scientist, measuring millimetre displacements of the Earth's surface with satellite-based and ground-based synthetic aperture radar interferometry (InSAR).

Photogrammetric Vision

3D positioning with satellite and close-range photogrammetry.
