Water/Flood Mapping App

An interactive web application for mapping waterbodies or floods anywhere in the world with radar and optical satellite imagery.
A highly efficient web platform for water pixel labeling in deep learning projects, ~2000x faster than a local workstation.

Introduction

I developed this web application based on Google Earth Engine originally for the purpose of mapping waterbodies or floods anywhere in the world with radar and optical satellite imagery. A demo is illustrated in the animation below.
